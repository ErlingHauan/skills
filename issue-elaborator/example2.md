# Decide product name for Altinity after Studio handover

## Summary

Decide on a user-facing product name for the multi-agent system (MAS) after ownership has been handed over to the Altinn Studio team.

Until then, "Altinity" remains an acceptable internal codename during development and beta.

## Context

The MAS is currently developed and evolved under the AI Lab with a strong technical focus. Naming at this stage would be premature, as:

- Long-term ownership will move to the Altinn Studio team
- The final product shape and user-facing positioning are not yet fixed
- Branding decisions should reflect product stewardship, not internal architecture

Earlier versions of this issue assumed active name exploration. That work is intentionally deferred.

## Intent

This issue exists to:

- Explicitly defer naming decisions until after beta
- Avoid premature or ad-hoc renaming during development
- Make ownership of the naming decision clear

## Ownership

This decision should be owned by the Altinn Studio team as part of productization after handover.

## Status

Post-beta, Studio-owned decision.

No naming work is planned or expected before beta.
